Non-Invasive Monitoring

The Smart Home Energy Monitoring System uses non-invasive current sensors to track energy usage per circuit (or lane) in your home. There is no need to tamper with appliances or modify internal wiring. The system is mounted safely at the distribution board and reads signals from the outside using safe, contactless methods.

  • No appliance-level wiring
  • No wall-breaking or rewiring
  • Mounted at DB/MCB with AI-based circuit profiling
  • Tamper-free and BIS-compliant design

Safe Installation

Our system attaches externally to your distribution board without requiring any cutting or splicing of electrical wires. This eliminates safety risks associated with traditional energy monitors.

Circuit-Level Monitoring

By monitoring at the circuit level, our system can track energy usage across your entire home without requiring individual smart plugs or appliance modifications.

AI-Based Recognition

Our proprietary algorithms analyze electrical signatures to identify individual appliances and their usage patterns, providing detailed insights without invasive monitoring.

Regulatory Compliant

Our system is engineered to comply with all applicable Indian regulations, including BIS safety standards (IS 13252) and WPC ETA certification for wireless communication. It is specifically designed for Indian residential electrical environments, accounting for typical power conditions, circuit layouts, and usage patterns. The device meets safety, electromagnetic compatibility (EMC), and wireless operation norms, ensuring safe, interference-free, and legally approved deployment in Indian homes.
